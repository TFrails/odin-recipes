Egg Sandwich

Description:

Breakfast lovers, rejoice! This is essentially just a grilled cheese sandwich with an omlette in the middle. Feel free to add the meat of your choosing, or don't! It's delicious by itself.

Thank me later.

Ingredients:

  • Bread, 2 slices
  • Butter, 1 tbsp
  • Mayonaise
  • Cheddar Cheese, 1-2 slices
  • Eggs, 2
  • Meat, optional

Steps:

  1. Start by preheating your skillet on medium low heat. Add butter and wait for it to begin to brown.
  2. Spread mayonaise on one side of each bread slice.
  3. Once butter has began to brown, place one slide of bread mayonaise-side down on the skillet.
  4. Place the cheese on the topside of the bread in the skillet to allow it to begin to melt.
  5. Place the other slice on top of the cheese, mayonaise-side up.
  6. While the first side of the sandwich is grilling, crack 1-2 eggs into a bowl.
  7. Use a fork to whisk vigorously until the egg mixture is smooth with a consistent texture.
  8. Flip the sandwich in the skillet to begin grilling the other side.
  9. Remove the sandwich from the skillet.
  10. If necessary, add more butter. Otherwise, pour in the eggs.
  11. Try to form the eggs into a square-ish shape approximately the size of your bread.
  12. Open the sandwich and scoop the eggs onto the cheese.
  13. At this point, add ham, sausage, or bacon as desired.
  14. Close the sandwich and enjoy!
