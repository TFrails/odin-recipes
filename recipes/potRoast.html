Mississippi Pot Roast

Description:

The Mississippi Pot Roast is perhaps my favorite slow cooker recipe. It is incredibly easy to make, as well as incredibly delicious. It's very easy to throw all the ingredients together before work, and it feels wonderful to come home and have a hot meal ready to go for dinner.

I found this recipe originally on Reddit. It has been my go-to for potlucks and similiar occasions for years. Read on to see how easy it is to create.

Ingredients:

  • Chuck Roast, 3.5lb
  • Butter, 1 stick
  • Au Jus Seasoning, 1 packet
  • Rach Seasoning, 1 packet
  • Whole Pepperoncinis, 8

Steps:

  1. Optionally, sear and brown the chuck roast realy well on all sides.
  2. Place the roast in a crock pot.
  3. Cover with all the ingredients listed above.
  4. Cook on low for 8 hours, or until tender.
  5. Consume mass quantities.
