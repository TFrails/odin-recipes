Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description:

The peanut butter and jelly sandwich is a classic American morsel that is quick and nutritious. It is a staple of my diet and that of my children.

If you need this page in order to successfully make this sandwich, I fear for your life.

Ingredients:

  • Bread, 2 slices
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps:

  1. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  2. Spread jelly on the other slice.
  3. Stack the two slices on top of each other so that the peanut butter and jelly are in the middle of the stack.
  4. Enjoy!
